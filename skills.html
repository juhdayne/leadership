What I've Learned

Communication & Collaboration

As a peer tutor, I’ve learned how to explain ideas clearly, ask good questions, and really listen. Working with Grade 9 students in English helped me realize that everyone learns differently, and it’s important to meet people where they’re at.

Time Management & Responsibility

Balancing school, volunteer work, and leadership responsibilities taught me how to manage my time. Whether it’s meeting a tutoring session or finishing assignments, I’ve learned how to set priorities and keep myself accountable.

Creativity & Technology

Whether it's making presentations, using digital tools, or managing social media, I enjoy using tech to connect and communicate. Being comfortable with technology has helped me collaborate and share ideas in new ways.
