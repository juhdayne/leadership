“Learning to Lead: My Journey Forward”

If I wrote a book about my life so far, I’d call it “Learning to Lead: My Journey Forward.” That title represents how leadership isn’t something you achieve overnight — it’s something you grow into, through mistakes, small wins, and reflection.

So far, my journey has included supporting others, learning from my community, and realizing that leadership is more about lifting people up than standing out. This portfolio is part of that journey — a chance to reflect on where I’ve been and where I want to go next.
